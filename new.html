Lynx Pro Audio line arrays at the Medusa Festival

Sep 8, 2023 | Touring, Touring and concerts

Medusa Sunbeach Festival, one of the benchmark EDM festivals in Spain with more than 300.000 attendees, has been held once again in Cullera (Valencia). With an spectacular line-up, this festival has welcomed some of the best electronic music DJ’s in the world, including famous celebrities such as Carl Cox, David Guetta, Paco Osuna, Amelie Lens, Dimitri Vegas, among many others.

For 3 days (and 2 days of pre-party), the concerts have continued non-stop between 6 in the afternoon and 7 in the morning. With a huge venue, the artists have been divided into 5 different stages with different music styles: EDM, techno, trap, dubstep or hardstyle.

The company specialized in shows, Maxim Sound, has provided the technical solution for Dragon Heart stage by Pepsi. For the main PA they have opted for LX series line arrays, with 8 LX-V12 enclosures on each side of the stage.

The LX-V12 is a very high output three-way self powered cabinet. With dual LF 12” (4” interleaved sandwich voice coil) neodymium woofers with double demodulating rings, four MF 6.5” transducers with glass fiber cones & ultra-light voice coil and two HF 1.4” neodymium magnet drivers with titanium diaphragm and individual high precision wave guide. DSP (FIR technology) controlled with 4000W amplification, 143dB SPL, built-in inclinometer.

With a very high power to size ratio and with easy rigging options offered the system can be taken from the truck and rigged up in record time. It is also very easy to use and control without the need for any external amps.

The LX-V12 cabinets have allowed a large number of customers to work at full power for years, thanks to their sturdiness and technology, designed by qualified engineers. Nowadays, in the Lynx Pro Audio catalog you will find other powerful and compact options such as the CLS series, which have improved both their external design and their internal technology.

Artists such as Anime, Angerfirst, Broken Minds, Raúl Ortiz, DJ Marta, among others, have performed on the Dragon Heart stage throughout the weekend.
